Deep Gandhi

I'm a Computer Science & Engineering graduate at Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Kottayam, India. My research interests lie in Multimodal Learning, Computer Vision and Generative AI. I have participated and achieved high rank in global AI competitions such as the Child Mind Institute - Problematic Internet Use and others as well as actively published open-source research tools such as datasets, notebooks on Kaggle. I'm particularly passionate about building models that can reason across visual and textual modalities enabling systems to understand, describe, and interact with the world more like humans do. I also aim to develop explainable, interpretable, and responsible AI systems that are both technically robust and aligned with human values. I am currently exploring opportunities to pursue graduate studies and research in Computer Vision and Multimodal Learning.
